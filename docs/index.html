Engineered for Performance

Focus Reader is designed to optimize how your brain processes text using scientifically proven methods.

RSVP Speed Engine

The core of the app. Our Rapid Serial Visual Presentation algorithm eliminates eye movement by displaying words at a fixed Optical Recognition Point (ORP). Read up to 1000 WPM.

  • Adjustable Speed Control
  • High-Contrast Dark Mode

Intelligent PDF Extractor

Bring your own books. We use the powerful PdfPig library to parse complex PDF documents instantly, stripping away distractions and formatting, serving you only the pure text content.

Interactive Practice

Training shouldn't be boring. Roll the 3D Dice to generate random educational snippets. Built with MAUI animations (`RotateTo`) and Async/Await patterns for a fluid experience.
