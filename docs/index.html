Master Your Cognitive Flow

Focus Reader isn't just an app; it's a tool engineered to optimize how your brain processes information.

High-Performance RSVP Engine

Say goodbye to subvocalization. Our Rapid Serial Visual Presentation algorithm displays words at a fixed Optical Recognition Point (ORP), allowing you to read up to 1000 words per minute without moving your eyes.

  • Adjustable WPM (Words Per Minute)
  • Dark Mode for reduced eye strain

Intelligent PDF Parsing

Powered by the robust PdfPig library, Focus Reader extracts pure text from complex PDF documents instantly. We strip away formatting and distractions, leaving only the content that matters.

  • Works with local files
  • Instant text extraction

Gamified Learning

Need practice material? Roll the 3D Dice. We use advanced animations (`RotateTo`) and async tasks to generate random educational snippets, making speed reading practice fun and engaging.
